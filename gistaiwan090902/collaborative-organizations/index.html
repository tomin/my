Collaborative Organizations
 

South American Business Forum

SABF is a student-run, non-profit organization, founded, directed and constituted by students from the Buenos Aires Institute of Technology (Hereafter referred to as ITBA) in 2004, without status of Legal Capacity, operating under the legal framework of ITBA. SABF organizes annually the first student-run international business conference for students from all over the World in South America, which aims to reduce the gap between future and present leaders, to achieve sustainable development of all nations.

 

International Conference on the Integration of Science and Technology

ICISTS-KAIST, short for International Conference on the Integration of Science and Technology, is a student-run conference, held in Daejeon, South Korea. ICISTS-KAIST was first held in the year 2005, and since then have attracted numerous renowned professionals in various fields, such as Jong-Wook Lee, former Director General of WHO, and Jim Dator, Director of the Hawaii Future Research Center. The goal of ICISTS-KAIST is to, most important of all, offer an open platform for non-professionals to freely discuss and share their ideas about pressing issues in science and technology.
