GIS Taiwan 2009 / Q & A
 
  1. How do I receive grant?
    2. Instructions of receiving the grants would be given after 25th April, in the second phase of registration. Precondition: participants must have full participation of the conference to receive grants.

  2. What if I can’t participate in every session of the conference?
    3. In order to receive the grant, it is required to attend at least the five-day conference. Pre-conference activities are optional but recommended.

  3. What should I do if I already have accommodation in Taipei?
    4. Participants who are accommodated independently are required to notify GIS Taiwan once qualifications are given to them.

  4. What are the exact days during which accommodations are provided?
    5. Accommodations are provided from 5th July to 11th July. Participants must check out on 12th July. Breakfast on 12th July is included.
